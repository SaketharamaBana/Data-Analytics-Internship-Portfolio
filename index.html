Key Insights

What the Data Revealed

Three chapters of business intelligence, each with a direct call to action.

📈

Revenue Growth Is Real

FY2024 revenue hit $861K — up 22.2% YoY. Q4 remains the seasonal peak, but 2024 showed consistent quarter-over-quarter improvement. Average order value grew 8.3% — signaling better product mix, not just volume.

👥

A Small Segment Drives Everything

Just 1,099 customers (Champions + Loyal, 46% of base) generate 93% of revenue. Champions average $758 per order. Meanwhile, 523 At-Risk customers have spent before and are reachable — worth $280K+ if re-engaged.

💎

Beauty Is the Hidden Gem

Electronics leads in revenue ($485K) but has the thinnest margin (22%). Beauty generates 55% gross margin — more than double Electronics. A budget reallocation of 15% toward Beauty could add $25K in high-margin revenue.

🌐

New Website Shows Promise

A/B test of the redesigned layout shows +15% relative conversion lift (10.96% → 12.60%). The result is directionally strong but not yet statistically significant at p=0.055. Extended testing is recommended before full rollout.

Statistical Validation

Hypotheses Tested with Rigor

Two formal statistical tests were performed to move from correlation to confidence.

✓ Rejected H₀

Revenue Growth — T-Test

H₀: Daily revenue in Q3 2024 = Q3 2023
H₁: Daily revenue in Q3 2024 is significantly higher

t = −7.63 · p < 0.0001

Conclusion: Revenue growth is statistically proven. 95% CI: +$662 to +$1,295 per day. This is not noise — it's real improvement.

⚠ Extend Test

Conversion Rate — Chi-Squared

H₀: New layout does not change conversion rate
H₁: New layout significantly increases conversion rate

χ² = 3.685 · p = 0.055

Conclusion: p = 0.055 is marginally above α = 0.05. The +15% lift is economically meaningful. Extend to 6,000+ visitors/group to reach statistical significance.
